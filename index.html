About the tinies

Small objects, strong personalities

Tiny Prophecy is a collection of handmade ceramic companions, each with its own mood, purpose, and slightly suspicious energy. Every piece is shaped by hand, making each one quietly unique.

Beneath the surface, each companion holds something waiting to be revealed. Bring your phone close and it will respond with a message meant just for you, sometimes comforting, sometimes unsettling, and occasionally a little too specific.

Some messages feel like reassurance, others like warnings, and a few land somewhere in between. They are not always helpful, but they are rarely easy to ignore.

Over time, these small interactions become part of a quiet routine. A moment of curiosity, a check-in, or a habit you didn't mean to start but somehow keep returning to.

Why people love them

Objects with a life of their own

Each piece is carefully shaped by hand using high-fired stoneware, giving it a solid, lasting quality while keeping its small, imperfect charm. No two are exactly alike, which makes each one feel a little more personal from the start.

What sets them apart is the combination of craft and interaction. These are not just decorative objects, but small companions designed to respond when approached. A simple gesture reveals something in return, turning a quiet object into a small, repeatable experience.

The collection comes from a mix of calm creation and slightly overactive imagination. Some pieces lean towards reassurance, others towards concern, and a few exist somewhere in between. Together, they form a strange but cohesive little world that people tend to come back to, share, and occasionally blame for new worries.
